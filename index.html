About

Ecodoc is a translation application that aims to provide a more inclusive learning environment for students by tranlsating economic terms into the official South African languages.


It is supported by funding from the University of Cape Town Teaching Grant Award

Features

Easy to use

Ecodoc has an intuitive interface that let's you easily search for economic terms.


1000+ economic terms

Ecodoc contains more than a 1000 terms. You can find the definition of these terms in multiple languages.

11+ languages

With more than 1000 economic terms, ecodoc lets you translate them into any of the 11 official South African languages.
